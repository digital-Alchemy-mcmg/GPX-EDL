# MARA / Diplomat

MARA / Diplomat receives only human-promoted observations. This stage owns candidate projection and outward-facing artifact generation. Diplomat is the presentation and synthesis face of the stage.

## Inputs

- receiving-side promotion object
- governed candidate source material
- artifact request

The exact promotion object schema is owned by the receiving side and must not be invented here.

## Outputs

- candidate projection
- outward-facing artifact, including supported resume, cover-letter, profile, or application-payload forms
- validation result required by the receiving implementation

## Authority limits

MARA / Diplomat may not source listings, bypass human promotion, or modify original evidence.

Projection and synthesis must remain grounded in the promoted observation and governed candidate material. Any receiving-side inference, approval, and versioning rules require their authoritative contracts before implementation.

## Liaison boundary

The completed artifact passes to the Liaison branch. Liaison is currently stubbed and user-executed; no autonomous send, submission, or ATS interaction is live.
